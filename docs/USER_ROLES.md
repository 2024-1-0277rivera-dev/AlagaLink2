*Focus: PDAO / MSWDO day-to-day operations and service governance.*

### 1. Member Management (Registry Control)
- **Full Registry Access**: View, search, and filter all registered members (Active, Pending, Suspended).
- **Manual Registration**: Register new members on their behalf using the comprehensive multi-section form.
- **Credential Editing**: Authority to modify IDEA categories, family composition, and medical data.

### 2. Evaluation Desk (Decision Authority)
- **Request Review**: Assess applicant credentials for ID, Devices, Medical, and PhilHealth requests.
- **Decision Workflow**: Approve or Reject requests with mandatory narrative justifications.

### 3. Inventory & Service Management
- **Catalog Management**: Add, edit, or remove entries for Assistive Devices, Medical Services, and Livelihood trainings.
- **Availability Control**: Toggle visibility of services based on current municipal stock or scheduling.

### 4. Lost & Found Reporting
- **Incident Profiling**: Authorized to create official community alerts for missing persons.

## ðŸ‘¤ Regular User (PWD/CWD Member)
*Focus: Personal data management and service availment.*

### 1. Personal Registry & Profile
- **Self-Verification**: View verified municipal registry data (IDEA Category, ID number, etc.).
- **Service History**: Access a centralized timeline of all availed programs.

### 2. Streamlined ID Portal
- **3-Step Application**: Confirm profile -> Select preferences -> Track status.
- **Digital Claims**: Access digital receipts and claim stubs for processed ID applications.

### 3. Programs & Inventory Access
- **Formal Enrollment**: Submit purpose-driven requests for Assistive Devices, Medical Aid, and Livelihood Workshops.
- **PhilHealth Portal**: Simple consent-based enrollment for sponsored health benefits.
